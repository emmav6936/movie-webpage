Solar High

A little bit about the film!

Watch our Trailer! You'll come running to theaters for the premier!

In a Universe, where how HOT you are depends on your proximity to the Sun: Two planets, one Burning Hot, and the other Sub-Zero temperatures, MEET. Through their mutual friend, the friendly and hospitable planet, Evie Earth, Virginia Venus and Noah Neptune fall in love and discover their compatibility despite protests from Steven Sun .

Evie Earth is the friendliest and most hospitable planet in Solar High. She is brilliant at making friends and often encourages others keep their relationship going although obstacles may arise on the way. Other planets in Solar High often get reffered to her for help!

Virginia Venus is the second closest planet to Steven Sun, after Mack Mercury, as well as the second smartest student of Solar High. She constantly receives an "A+" in school and due to her resolute desires to succeed in her studies, she was never given a chance to love. However, with the help of Evie Earth, she grows close to another student at Solar High. The connection between the two, despite their differences, cannot be broken!

Noah Neptune is the coolest planet in Solar High. All others bow down at his feet when they see him because he rules the school. Girl planets are always all over him, asking for his number and of course to take them out. However, once he meets Virginia Venus, he believes that she is the girl of his dreams and therefore, changes his outlook on life. His new goal in school, is not to get as many girls as he can, but it's to improve his grades so that Virginia Venus would agree to go out with him!

Steven Sun is the principal of Solar High. Little did we know, he is also the proud father of Virginia Venus. Crazy Right? Well, he knows that Noah Neptune isn't a good guy, but a player, therefore he forbids his daughter to date him. That remains his stance until Noah Neptune actual begins to change for the better. He turns into a respectful, mature, and intelligent young planet awaiting Steven Sun's approval to date Virginia. Steven Sun has his doubts, but agrees that Noah did change for the better and allows Noah to date his daughter.
